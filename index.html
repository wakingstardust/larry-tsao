Hi, I’m Larry!

I’m a second-year college student at University of California, Riverside currently working towards my Bachelors of Arts in Sociology.

In the graphic design field, I’ve designed posters, buttons, book covers, and branding both professionally and as personal projects. At Stream of Praise Music Ministries, a Christian Mandarin-Chinese worship group, I have directed various productions as lighting director as well as intern as an administrative assistant in Spring 2020. I still assist various productions as lighting director as they arise.

In my free time, you’ll find me reading a good sci-fi book, playing video games, or listening to music. As for my passions, they reside within various creative and technical fields. I love tinkering and working on various personal projects, from the creative, like graphic design, video editing, lighting design, to the deeply technical, like pc building, jailbreaking, and various other bits and pieces I’ve dabbled in over the years.
